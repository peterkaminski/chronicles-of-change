A reading list of books that discuss the collapse of societies, as well as insights into how societies adapt, transform, or reinvent themselves in the face of challenges. Sorted alphabetically by title. Publication year is bolded for ease of identification.

To comment, edit, or add content, or to download as a spreadsheet, use the Google Sheets version: Books About the Collapse, Transformation, and Reinvention of Societies. The spreadsheet is used to build this document. Contact a maintainer to have this document refreshed from the spreadsheet.

The links in the book titles will take you to GoodReads.

Descriptions are from various sources: from people who have read the book, from GoodReads, and some were generated by ChatGPT (GPT-4, v2023-08-03).
